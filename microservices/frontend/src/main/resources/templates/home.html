The traffic coordinator

Where requests get organised. Handles fast sync calls and prepares async work when needed.

Why it matters: keeps flows predictable while the platform grows.

Kafka Worker

The background doer

Where queued jobs stop pretending to be magic and actually get processed without blocking the UI.

Why it matters: decouples long-running work from user-facing speed.
